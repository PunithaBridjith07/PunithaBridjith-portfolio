About Me

I am a self-motivated and detail-oriented Full Stack Developer with practical experience in building dynamic and scalable web applications. I specialize in frontend development using React.js and Angular.js and backend systems powered by Django and Node.js, with database management in PostgreSQL and CouchDB.

I recently completed a 6-month internship at Chain-Sys India Pvt Ltd, where I worked on real-time modules, integrated backend APIs, and improved UI features across various components. My previous internships have involved developing internal tools, working with RESTful services, and deploying applications with best practices.

My projects reflect my passion for solving real-world problems through software — including a document scanner with a trial/subscription model, a smart note-taking application with integrated search, and a QR-based ticketing and validation system. These experiences have refined my ability to work independently and in collaborative environments.

I’m enthusiastic about continuous learning and am always ready to embrace new technologies, contribute to innovative solutions, and grow as a developer in fast-paced, evolving environments.
